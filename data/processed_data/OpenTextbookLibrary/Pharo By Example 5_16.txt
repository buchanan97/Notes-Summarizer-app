You can find the location in the Pharo Launcher settings. The World Menu Once Pharo is running, you should see a single large window, possibly containing some open playground windows (see Figure - ). You might notice a menu bar, but Pharo mainly makes use of context-dependent pop-up menus. Clicking anywhere on the background of the Pharo window will display the World Menu , which contains many of the Pharo tools, utilities and settings. At the top of the World Menu , you will see a list of several core tools in Pharo, including the System Browser, the Playground, the Monticello package manager, and others. We will discuss them in more detail in the coming chapters.
