Students should walk away with skills that they can take into the other disciplines. The course requires mas tery of a number of software skills primarily from the Microsoft Office suite. These include skills in Power Point, Word, and Excel. We assumed no prior background knowledge on the part of the students. Our experience is that students entering college have exposure to software skills, but not a mastery of applying those skills to solve business problems. A number of skills are also learned about cloud computing. These include Web site design and development (Google Sites, Google Gadgets, Google Docs), Color Management (Adobe Kuler Color), i Phone App mockups (Mock App), and online polls (Poll Everywhere). The book was designed for both in class and online delivery and for small and large section sizes. The non - traditional student population is a growing sector a nd many of those students choose to learn online.
