About the Textbook Web development is an evolving amalgamation of languages that work in concert to re - ceive, modify, and deliver information between parties using the Internet as a mechanism of delivery. While it is easy to describe conceptually, implementation is accompanied by an overwhelming variety of languages, platforms, templates, frameworks, guidelines, and stan - dards. Navigating a project from concept to completion often requires more than mastery of one or two complementing languages, meaning today s developers need both breadth, and depth, of knowledge to be effective. This text provides the developer with an understanding of the various elements of web development by focusing on the concepts and fundamentals through the examples within, providing a foundation that allows easier transition to other languages and a better un - derstanding of how to approach their work.
