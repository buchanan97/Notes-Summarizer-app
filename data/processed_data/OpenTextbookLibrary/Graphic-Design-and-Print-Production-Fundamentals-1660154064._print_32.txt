The communication must carry a specific message to a specific audience on behalf of the client, and do so ef fectively usually within the container of a concept that creates context and builds interest for the project in the viewer . See an illustrated model of the design process here: A Model of the Creative Process Overview of the Design Process The process of developing ef fective design is complex. It begins with research and the definition of project goals. Defining goals allows you to home in on precisely what to communicate and who the audience is. Y ou can then appropriately craft the message you are trying to communicate to them. Additional information regarding how to deliver your message and why it s necessary are also clarified in the research stage.
