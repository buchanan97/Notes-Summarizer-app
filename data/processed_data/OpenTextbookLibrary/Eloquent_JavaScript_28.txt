It is followed by the name of the binding and, if we want to immediately give it a value, by an operator and an expression. The example creates a binding called caughtand uses it to grab hold of the number that is produced by multiplying by . After a binding has been defined, its name can be used as an expression. The value of such an expression is the value the binding currently holds. Here s an example: let ten ; console.log(ten ten); When a binding points at a value, that does not mean it is tied to that value forever.
