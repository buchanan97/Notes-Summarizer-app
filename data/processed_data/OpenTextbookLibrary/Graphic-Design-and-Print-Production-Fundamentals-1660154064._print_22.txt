Guiding Principles ITS was built on the shoulders of the less is more ideal of the German W erkbund and the Bauhaus school. But its pioneers pursued ideologies that had much more depth and subtlety . Ernst Keller , whose work in design spanned over four decades, brought an approach to problem solving that was unique. His contribution to design was in defining the problem. For Keller , the solution to a design problem rested in its content. Content-driven design is now a standard practice.
