In addition the system supports the following mouse shortcuts CMD-Click on a word: open the definition of a class when the word is a class name. You get also the implementors of the message when you click on a selector that is in the body of a method. CMD-Shift-Click on a word: open a list browser with all the refs of the class when the word is a class name.
